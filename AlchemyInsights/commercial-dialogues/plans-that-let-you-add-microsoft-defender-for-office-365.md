# <a name="plans-that-let-you-add-microsoft-defender-for-office-365"></a>Paketi, ki omogočajo dodajanje Microsoftovega zagovornika za Office 365

Če imate enega od teh paketov, lahko dodate Microsoft Defender za Office 365:

- Exchange Online (paket 1 ali 2)
- Kiosk za Exchange Online
- Exchange Online Protection
- Office 365 Business Essentials ali Business Premium
- Office 365 Enterprise E1, E3 ali F1
- Office 365 a1 ali a3
