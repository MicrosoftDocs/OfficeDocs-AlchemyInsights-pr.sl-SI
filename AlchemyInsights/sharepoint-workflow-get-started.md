# <a name="workflows-in-sharepoint"></a>Poteki dela v SharePointu

Poteki dela v SharePointu omogočajo modeliranje in avtomatizacijo poslovnih procesov. SharePoint podpira dva osnovna okolja za razvoj poteka dela za avtorske poteke dela: SharePoint Designer in Visual Studio. 

Glejte [razvijanje SharePointovih potekov dela v programu SharePoint Designer in Visio](https://docs.microsoft.com/sharepoint/dev/general-development/develop-sharepoint-workflows-using-visual-studio) ali [razvijanje SharePointovih potekov dela z Visual Studiem](https://docs.microsoft.com/sharepoint/dev/general-development/develop-sharepoint-workflows-using-visual-studio) , s katerim lahko ocenite prednosti in slabosti posameznih. 

Če poskušate ugotoviti, ali so poteki dela rešitev za uporabo v SharePointovem okolju ali če iščete splošne informacije o začetku, si oglejte [pregled potekov dela v SharePointu](https://docs.microsoft.com/sharepoint/dev/general-development/get-started-with-workflows-in-sharepoint#overview-of-workflows-in-sharepoint).
