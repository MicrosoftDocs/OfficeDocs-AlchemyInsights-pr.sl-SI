# <a name="offboarding-machines-from-the-microsoft-defender-atp-service"></a>Offboarding machines from the Microsoft Defender ATP service

Če potrebujete pomoč pri prenosu računalnika iz storitve Defender ATP, si oglejte navodila za operacijski sistem:  

- Za Windows 10 glejte [Offboard devices from the Microsoft Defender ATP service](/windows/security/threat-protection/microsoft-defender-atp/offboard-machines#offboard-windows-10-devices).
- Če ne Windows, glejte [Izklop naprav, ki niso Windows.](/windows/security/threat-protection/microsoft-defender-atp/configure-endpoints-non-windows#offboard-non-windows-devices)
