# <a name="offboarding-machines-from-the-microsoft-defender-atp-service"></a>Prenašanje oddaljenim stroji storitve Microsoft Defender ATP

Če potrebujete pomoč pri prenašanje oddaljenim računalnika s storitvijo storitve ATP Defender, glejte navodila za vaš operacijski sistem:  

- V napravah s sistemom Windows 10 si oglejte [offboard naprave v storitvi Microsoft DEFENDER ATP](https://docs.microsoft.com/windows/security/threat-protection/microsoft-defender-atp/offboard-machines#offboard-windows-10-devices).
- V napravah, ki niso v sistemu Windows, glejte [naprave, ki niso v sistemu Windows offboard](https://docs.microsoft.com/windows/security/threat-protection/microsoft-defender-atp/configure-endpoints-non-windows#offboard-non-windows-devices).
