- [Izklop, onemogočanje ali odstranjevanje OneDrive](https://support.office.com/article/turn-off-disable-or-uninstall-onedrive-f32a17ce-3336-40fe-9c38-6efb09f944b0)

- [Onemogočanje ustvarjanja mesta OneDrive za nekatere uporabnike](https://docs.microsoft.com/sharepoint/manage-user-profiles#disable-onedrive-creation-for-some-users)
- [Nadzor dostopa do podatkov v storitvi SharePoint online in OneDrive na podlagi omrežnega mesta](https://docs.microsoft.com/sharepoint/control-access-based-on-network-location)
- [Preprečite uporabnikom sinhronizacijo osebnih računov za OneDrive](https://docs.microsoft.com/onedrive/use-group-policy#DisablePersonalSync)
