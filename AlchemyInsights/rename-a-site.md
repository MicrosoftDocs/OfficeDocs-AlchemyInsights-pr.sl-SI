# <a name="rename-a-sharepoint-site"></a>Preimenovanje SharePointovega mesta

Sledite korakom v [tem članku](https://docs.microsoft.com/sharepoint/change-site-address) , da spremenite ime mesta-na primer, od https://contoso.sharepoint.com/sites/sample1 to. https://contoso.sharepoint.com/sites/sample2

**Opomba:** Imena domene (v zgornjem primeru, dela URL-ja) ne morete spremeniti v URL-ju SharePoint online. 

Če želite več informacij, glejte:

- [Spreminjanje imena domene SharePoint](https://go.microsoft.com/fwlink/?Linkid=2018696)
- [Napake, ko preimenujete naslov SharePointovega mesta](https://support.office.com/article/errors-when-you-rename-a-sharepoint-site-address-165b7c11-1325-4813-b160-ecbe87bc1a86)
