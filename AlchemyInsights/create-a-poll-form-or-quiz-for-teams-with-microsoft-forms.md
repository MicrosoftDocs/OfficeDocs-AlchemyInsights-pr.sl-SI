 - [Ustvarjajte, urejajte in sodelujte](https://support.office.com/article/create-edit-and-collaborate-on-a-form-in-microsoft-teams-333b97a3-41d9-48bc-a1cb-84a96bd44e14) v obrazcu v aplikaciji Microsoft Teams.
