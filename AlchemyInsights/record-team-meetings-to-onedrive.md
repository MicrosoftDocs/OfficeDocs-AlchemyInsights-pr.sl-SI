# <a name="teams-meeting-recordings-to-onedrive"></a>Posnetki srečanja v OneDrive

Microsoft Teams ima nov način shranjevanja posnetkov srečanja. Kot prva faza prehoda od klasičnega Microsoftovega toka do [novega toka](https://docs.microsoft.com/stream/streamnew/new-stream), ta način shranjuje posnetke v Microsoft OneDrive za podjetja in SharePoint v programu Microsoft 365 in ponuja številne prednosti.  

- [Nastavitev možnosti snemanja srečanja za OneDrive za podjetja in SharePoint](https://docs.microsoft.com/MicrosoftTeams/tmr-meeting-recording-change#set-up-the-meeting-recording-option-for-onedrive-for-business-and-sharepoint)
- [Onemogočanje OneDrive za podjetja in SharePoint za nadaljnjo uporabo toka](https://docs.microsoft.com/MicrosoftTeams/tmr-meeting-recording-change#opt-out-of-onedrive-for-business-and-sharepoint-to-continue-using-stream)  
- [Pogosta vprašanja](https://docs.microsoft.com/MicrosoftTeams/tmr-meeting-recording-change#frequently-asked-questions)
